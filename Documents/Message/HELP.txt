Commands:
- DISCONNECT : disconnects player.
- GETPLAYERS : show the list of connected player.
- CHALLENGE <targetPlayer> : extend a match invitation to targeted player.
- ACCEPT <y\n> : accept or refuse recived invitation
- PLAY <cardNum> : play card number cardNum from your hand
- SURRENDER : surrender victory of the current match to the opponent
- MATCH_MSG <message>: send a message to the opponent
- RULES : show game Rules
- HELP : show this list of commands
